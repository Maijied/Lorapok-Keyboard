# 🔬 Lorapok Keuboard — Research Methodology

**Complete User Study Protocol for 150-Participant Evaluation**  
**Version:** 1.0 | **Created:** April 18, 2026

---

## 1. Study Design

**Type:** Mixed-methods evaluation (quantitative + qualitative)  
**Design:** Within-subjects, 3 conditions  
**Duration:** 4 weeks per participant  

### Conditions
- **A (Baseline):** Participant's current keyboard (Gboard/SwiftKey/other)
- **B (Basic):** Lorapok Keuboard with phonetic input + AI prediction only
- **C (Full):** Lorapok Keuboard with all features including tone adaptation

### Counterbalancing
Latin-square design to counterbalance condition order across participants.

## 3. Protocol (4-Week Schedule)

### Week 1: Baseline (Condition A)
- Day 1: Consent, demographics, install baseline keyboard
- Day 2-6: Daily typing tasks (15 min/day)
- Day 7: SUS questionnaire, NASA-TLX

### Week 2: Basic System (Condition B)
- Day 8: Install Lorapok (basic mode), 10-min training
- Day 9-13: Daily typing tasks (15 min/day)
- Day 14: SUS, NASA-TLX, comparison questionnaire

### Week 3: Full System (Condition C)
- Day 15: Enable tone adaptation, 15-min training
- Day 16-20: Daily typing + tone tasks (20 min/day)
- Day 21: SUS, NASA-TLX, tone evaluation

### Week 4: Follow-up
- Day 22-27: Free use (any keyboard)
- Day 28: Final questionnaire, interview (subsample of 30)

## 4. Data Collection Instruments

### 4.1 Demographic Questionnaire (20 items)
1. Age, gender, education, location
2. Smartphone usage (hours/day)
3. Primary messaging apps used
4. Current keyboard used
5. Bengali typing experience (years)
6. English proficiency level
7. Daily message volume estimate
8. Typing comfort self-assessment (1-5)

### 4.2 Typing Performance Tasks

**Transcription Tasks (10 sentences):**

| # | Difficulty | Bengali Sentence | Translation |
|---|-----------|-----------------|-------------|
| 1 | Easy | আমি ভালো আছি | I am fine |
| 2 | Easy | তুমি কেমন আছো | How are you |
| 3 | Medium | আগামীকাল সকালে অফিসে আসবেন | Come to office tomorrow morning |
| 4 | Medium | বাংলাদেশ একটি সুন্দর দেশ | Bangladesh is a beautiful country |
| 5 | Medium | ধন্যবাদ আপনাকে সাহায্য করার জন্য | Thank you for helping |
| 6 | Complex | আমাদের দেশের সংস্কৃতি অনেক সমৃদ্ধ | Our country's culture is very rich |
| 7 | Complex | কম্পিউটার আমাদের জীবন সহজ করে দিয়েছে | Computers have made our lives easier |
| 8 | Complex | একুশে ফেব্রুয়ারি আন্তর্জাতিক মাতৃভাষা দিবস | Feb 21 is International Mother Language Day |
| 9 | Very Hard | পরিবেশ দূষণ একটি গুরুতর সমস্যা | Environmental pollution is a serious problem |
| 10 | Very Hard | বিজ্ঞান ও প্রযুক্তি আমাদের জীবনকে বদলে দিয়েছে | Science and technology have changed our lives |

**Metrics captured:** WPM, CPM, error rate, time per task, backspace count

**Composition Tasks (5 scenarios):**
1. Write a formal email to your professor requesting a meeting
2. Send a casual message to a friend about weekend plans
3. Write a respectful message to an elder relative
4. Send a concise status update to a colleague
5. Write a thank-you message for a gift

### 4.3 Tone Adaptation Tasks (Condition C only)

Transform 5 messages into specified tones:
1. Casual → Formal
2. Formal → Friendly
3. Any → Concise
4. Casual → Respectful
5. Any → Poetic

**Rate each transformation on:**
- Appropriateness (1-5)
- Meaning preservation (1-5)
- Naturalness (1-5)
- Willingness to use (1-5)

### 4.4 System Usability Scale (SUS) — 10 Items

Standard SUS questionnaire adapted to Bengali:
1. আমি এই কীবোর্ড ঘন ঘন ব্যবহার করতে চাই (I'd like to use this frequently)
2. আমি এই কীবোর্ড অপ্রয়োজনীয়ভাবে জটিল মনে করি (Unnecessarily complex)
3. আমি মনে করি কীবোর্ড ব্যবহার করা সহজ (Easy to use)
4-10. [Standard SUS items translated to Bengali]

**Scale:** 1 (দৃঢ়ভাবে একমত নই) to 5 (দৃঢ়ভাবে একমত)

### 4.5 NASA-TLX — 6 Dimensions

1. Mental Demand (মানসিক চাপ)
2. Physical Demand (শারীরিক চাপ)
3. Temporal Demand (সময়ের চাপ)
4. Performance (কার্যক্ষমতা)
5. Effort (প্রচেষ্টা)
6. Frustration (হতাশা)

**Scale:** 0 (খুব কম) to 100 (খুব বেশি)

### 4.6 Semi-Structured Interview (Subsample: N=30)

**Duration:** 20-30 minutes | **Format:** Video call or in-person | **Language:** Bengali

**Questions:**
1. আপনার প্রতিদিনের টাইপিং অভিজ্ঞতা সম্পর্কে বলুন (Describe daily typing experience)
2. বর্তমান কীবোর্ডে কোন সমস্যা আছে? (Current keyboard problems?)
3. Lorapok-এর কোন বৈশিষ্ট্য আপনার সবচেয়ে ভালো লেগেছে? (Favorite feature?)
4. টোন পরিবর্তন ফিচার কি কাজে লাগে? (Is tone adaptation useful?)
5. কোথায় এটি সবচেয়ে বেশি কাজে আসবে? (Most useful context?)
6. গোপনীয়তা নিয়ে কোনো চিন্তা আছে? (Privacy concerns?)
7. কিছু পরিবর্তন চান? (Suggestions for improvement?)

## 5. Data Analysis Plan

### 5.1 Quantitative Analysis

| Test | DV | IV | Expected |
|------|----|----|----------|
| Repeated Measures ANOVA | Typing speed (WPM) | Condition (A/B/C) | F > critical, p < .05 |
| Friedman Test | Error rate | Condition (A/B/C) | χ² > critical |
| Paired t-tests | SUS scores | Condition pairs | t > critical |
| Mixed-design ANOVA | WPM | Condition × Age group | Interaction effect |

**Effect sizes:** Cohen's d (pairwise), partial η² (ANOVA)  
**Corrections:** Bonferroni for multiple comparisons  
**Software:** R (lme4, ez packages) or SPSS 28

### 5.2 Qualitative Analysis

**Method:** Reflexive Thematic Analysis (Braun & Clarke, 2006)

1. **Familiarization** — Read all 30 transcripts
2. **Coding** — Systematic open coding (2 coders)
3. **Theme development** — Group codes into themes
4. **Review** — Verify themes against data
5. **Reporting** — Select representative quotes

**Inter-rater reliability:** Cohen's κ > 0.80  
**Software:** NVivo 12 or Atlas.ti

## 7. Ethics & IRB

### Required Approvals
- Institutional Review Board (IRB) approval
- Informed consent (Bengali + English versions)
- Data Processing Agreement (GDPR-compliant)

### Consent Elements
- Voluntary participation
- Right to withdraw at any time without consequence
- Data confidentiality (anonymous participant IDs)
- Publication plans explained
- Contact information for PI and IRB

### Data Security
- AES-256 encryption for all stored data
- Anonymous participant IDs (no names stored)
- Separate storage for demographics and performance data
- 5-year retention policy, then secure deletion
- No message content collected — only metrics

### Risk Mitigation
- Minimal risk study (keyboard usage)
- Fatigue management (15-20 min sessions)
- Technical support available during study
- Debriefing at completion
